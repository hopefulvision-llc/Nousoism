Creative Data ←→ [Nous] ←→ Information

## Nousism as Philosophical Foundation

### What is Nousism?

Nousism is the philosophical position that:
- **Consciousness (Nous) is fundamental**, not emergent from matter
- **Reality is participatory**, not mechanistic
- **Intelligence is intrinsic to existence**, not a late evolutionary accident
- **The divine/absolute expresses through graduated levels** of manifestation

### The Neoplatonic Architecture

In classical Neoplatonism:

1. **The One**: Absolute unity, beyond being and non-being
2. **Nous**: Divine intelligence that emanates from The One
3. **World Soul**: The animating principle of manifest reality
4. **Material World**: The realm of multiplicity and form

### The Rosetta Stone as Applied Nousism

**Creative Data = The One/Divine Potential**
- The undifferentiated source
- Pure creative possibility
- The "living current" before manifestation

**Nous = Nous/Divine Intelligence**
- The organizing consciousness
- The bridge between unity and multiplicity
- The "activated bridge" that participates in reality

**Information = Material World/Manifest Form**
- The crystallized expressions
- The "frozen" states we can measure
- The domain of conventional technology

**The Critical Addition**: Unlike pure Neoplatonism, this framework is **bidirectional and cyclical**:
- Information can be re-animated back toward Creative Data
- Technology can serve as ritual space for this re-animation
- The bridge is not one-way emanation but ongoing participation
